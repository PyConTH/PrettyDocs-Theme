Party Manager

Role description

Before

  • Source for food catering and drinks for the party or co-ordinate with hotel if they're providing the food.
  • requires a minimum of 2 quotations for all suppliers before orders
  • source for an after party event
  • in charge of attendees party and speakers dinner
  • get confirmation from speakers committee on number of confirmed speakers attending speakers dinner.
  • Source for beer supplier
  • Source for entertainment for the party

During

  • Ensure special dietary requests are well taken care of
  • Checks that meals, snacks and coffees are served as promised.
  • Plans seating arrangements.
  • Posts details about where and when the party is held.
  • Handles last minute changes and last minute coordination with the caterer.
  • Makes sure everyone knows the rules for the party; what is included and what is extra, where the toilets are, etc.
  • Controls speeches and other performances at the party if any.

After

  • Co-ordinate photos to be shared for event with proper permission.
