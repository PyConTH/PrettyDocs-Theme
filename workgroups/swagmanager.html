Swag Manager

Role description

Preferably someone who can speak Thai

Before event:

  • Get designs from design team for stickers, t-shirts, and other swags.
  • handles t-shirt production, stickers production, and all other souvenirs.
  • Coordinate with all swag producers.
  • Determines t-shirt production estimation through attendees count received from logistics lead.
  • Prepares swag orders. Picks up the materials, counts them, sorts them and delivers them to the registration desk for handing out to participants. Produces a written instruction for the distribution.
  • settle payment for swags

During

  • Ensures all swags delivered and handled to Handout lead onsite.

Guidelines

Swag is not limited to t-shirts and tote bags. There's an entire industry out there devoted to making people smile at conferences (or making their kids smile when they get home).
