Last updated: September 19th, 2019

Marketing Committee

Role description

Throughout entire event:

Before event:

  • Ensure marketing contents are scheduled and spread evenly
  • Create timeline to spread contents evenly in advance. Consider using buffer.
  • Create an event on Facebook

During

  • allocate team members to post live videos, contents, tweets about events. Constant reminder on using official hashtag for event.

After

  • Follow-up with team on their work and evaluate on what needs to be improved for next year's content.

Guidelines

Press

  • Contact press about conference 3-6 months before conference, suggesting they attend
  • Issue one or more press before the conference
  • Who will you target during these releases?
  • Prepared press releases one week before their release date, and ensures translators translate them during this time.
  • Get contacts for all sponsors
  • Get contacts for all reporters
  • Arrange press badges for journalists (they like that kind of thing)
  • Meet journalists personally and go over any last minute schedule changes
  • Ring attending press 1 month before conference, review schedule with them, and identify speakers they might be interested in interviewing
  • Make a press room available with computer, desk, table for interviews, and coffee station if possible.
  • On site: manage interview schedules (try to ensure that if the same person is being interviewed 3 times that they happen consecutively, and

contact them before the conference to identify the time that suits best)

All of the press stuff probably needs a full-time volunteer during the conference, and a lot of time over a couple of months before the conference.
