INTRODUCTION

About PyCon Thailand

PyCon Thailand is a community effort driven by the members of the Python Community in Bangkok and the entire organising team are all volunteers. PyCon Thailand sets itself apart from many other developer events in Thailand by being both non-profit and by having a ticket price the event can be a professional conference that provides valuable training and networking to companies. Our mission is to create a networking channel Any money made from ticket sales or sponsorship is put back into future events and to grow the community.

The purpose of this handbook is to work as a space for collaborative editing:

1. To manage PyCon working groups;

2. For initial internal development of proposals and plans, that may not yet be ready to be shared publicly;

3. To keep all current active committee members on track with roles and responsibilities;

4. To serve as a guidance for all new committee members involved in the event.
